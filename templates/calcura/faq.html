Frequently asked questions

What types of graphing calculators are available for sale on Calcura?

It all depends on our vendors! The diversity of calculators you can buy depends on which calculators our vendors have to sell; however, you can always expect a TI-84 to be on sale.

Are the used graphing calculators sold on Calcura in good condition?

When shopping for a used graphing calculator on Calcura, it's important to keep in mind that each device has its unique history, ranging from rarely used to having been reintroduced to the market multiple times. To ensure a smooth buying experience, we encourage our customers to communicate clearly with sellers and carefully review all product images before making a purchase.

Can I sell my own used graphing calculator on Calcura?

Of course! Vendors are the backbone of the website ensuring that a constant flow of calculators enters the market. Under your profile image, go to the vendor page to create your first listing.

How do I make a payment on Calcura?

Due to legality issues, you are not able to make payments on Calcura. Instead, consumers are to meet with vendors at a location which they both agree on.

Why are only ocdsb accounts allowed on calcura? Why can't I use my gmail account.

By limiting users to their school accounts, we can prevent the creation of fraudulent accounts on our platform. This helps to ensure that any harmful behavior is quickly identified and prevented from happening again. If everyone were allowed to use their Gmail accounts, the risk of fraudulent activity would be much higher, as users can continuously make accounts for fradulent behaviour.

Are there any fees associated with selling on Calcura?

There are no fees associated with selling on Calcura, which means that you can list your used graphing calculators for sale and connect with potential buyers without having to worry about paying any commissions or fees. Simply create an account, list your item, and start selling!

What should I do if a potential buyer does not show up to a scheduled meeting?

Try setting up another meeting with them. If they continuously do not show up, feel free to report them within the chat room.

How do I delete or edit my listing if my calculator has already been sold?

Under your dashboard head to the vendorpage. Click on the listing you want to delete. There, you will see the edit listing page, where you can edit fields of a listing or delete it entirely.
